# Xulq-atvor kodeksi | Code of Conduct

## Bizning majburiyatimiz

Biz — loyiha a'zolari, hissadorlar va rahbarlar — hammaga — yoshi, jinsidan qat'i nazar,
nogiron yoki nogiron emas, etnik kelib chiqishi, jinsiy yo'nalishi, tajribasi, millati,
tashqi ko'rinishi, irqi, dini yoki ijtimoiy va iqtisodiy holatidan qat'i nazar — bezovtalik
va tahqirlanishdan xoli, ochiq va mehribon muhitda ishtirok etishni ta'minlashga va'da beramiz.

Biz xatti-harakatlarimiz va o'zaro munosabatlarimiz orqali inklyuziv, xush muomala va sog'lom
hamjamiyatni tiklashga intilamiz.

## Bizning standartlarimiz

Hamjamiyatimizda ijobiy muhitni yaratishga yordam beradigan xatti-harakatlar:

* Boshqa odamlarga nisbatan hamdard va hurmat bilan munosabatda bo'lish
* Turli fikrlar, nuqtai nazarlar va tajribalarga hurmat bilan yondashish
* Konstruktiv tanqidni munosib qabul qilish va berish
* Xatolarimiz ta'sirida bo'lgan shaxslar oldida javobgarlikni his qilish va uzr so'rash,
  va bu tajribadan o'rganish
* Faqat o'zimizga emas, balki butun hamjamiyatga nima foydali ekanligiga e'tibor berish

Qabul qilinmaydigan xatti-harakatlar:

* Jinsiy xarakterli til va tasvirlar, va har qanday turdagi jinsiy e'tibor yoki suiiste'mol
* Trolling, haqoratli yoki tahqirlovchi izohlar va shaxsiy yoki siyosiy hujumlar
* Ommaviy yoki xususiy ta'qib
* Aniq ruxsatsiz boshqalarning xususiy ma'lumotlarini (jismoniy manzil, elektron pochta) e'lon qilish
* Professional muhitda nomaqbul deb hisoblash mumkin bo'lgan boshqa xatti-harakatlar

## Ta'minlash majburiyatlari

Jamiyat rahbarlari xulq-atvor standartlarini aniqlashtirish va amalga oshirish uchun javobgardir
va har qanday nomaqbul, tahqirlovchi, xavfli yoki zararli xatti-harakatlarga nisbatan tegishli va
adolatli tuzatuvchi choralar ko'radi.

## Qo'llanish doirasi

Ushbu Xulq-atvor kodeksi barcha hamjamiyat maydonlarida va shaxs rasmiy ravishda
hamjamiyatni ifodalagan vaqtda ochiq maydonda qo'llaniladi.

## Ta'minlash

Suiiste'mol qilish, ta'qib qilish yoki boshqa qabul qilinmaydigan xatti-harakatlar haqida
loyiha rahbarlari bilan bog'liq GitHub Issues orqali xabar bering. Barcha shikoyatlar tezda
va adolatli ko'rib chiqiladi.

# Code of Conduct (English)

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a
harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible
disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience,
education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion,
or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

* Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward other people
* Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
* Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
* Accepting responsibility and apologizing to those affected by our mistakes
* Focusing on what is best not just for us as individuals, but for the overall community

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

* The use of sexualized language or imagery, and sexual attention or advances of any kind
* Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
* Public or private harassment
* Publishing others' private information without their explicit permission
* Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable
behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior that
they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.
